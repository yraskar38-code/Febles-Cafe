Our Story

A chapter written with coffee

Every great café has a founding myth. Ours begins with two friends, a borrowed espresso machine, and a shelf of dog-eared novels.

The Beginning

From a single shelf to a sanctuary

✦

It started in 2019, when childhood friends Aryan Kapoor and Meera Nair turned a crumbling Bandra bookshop into something the neighbourhood didn't know it was missing.

Meera had trained as a barista in Vienna. Aryan had spent a decade curating rare books. Together, they imagined a place where the pleasure of a perfect cup met the slow joy of a good story.

They painted the walls the colour of old parchment, lined them with secondhand novels, and opened the doors on a monsoon morning in August 2019. Forty-three people came. Most of them stayed for hours.

2019

Founded

Opened our doors during the Mumbai monsoon with a single espresso machine and 400 books.

2021

Garden Room Opens

We expanded into the courtyard, creating our beloved outdoor terrace with trailing jasmine.

2023

Best Independent Café

Awarded Mumbai's Best Independent Café by Condé Nast Traveller India.

Today

Still Telling Stories

We now host 2,000+ guests a week, host 3 reading circles, and roast our own coffee.

What We Believe

The Fables Philosophy

I.

Slowness is a virtue

We don't do take-away queues. We do lingering mornings. Come when you have time to taste something properly.

II.

Coffee is craft

Every origin we source, every roast profile we develop, is guided by a simple question: does this tell a better story?

III.

Community over commerce

We host free reading circles every Sunday. Our shelves are a lending library. We believe books and coffee should reach everyone.

IV.

Honesty in every cup

No artificial syrups. No supermarket beans. We will always tell you exactly where your coffee comes from and how it was made.
